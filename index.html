About Proxuma Lite

Proxuma Lite provides on-device threat evaluation for URLs and QR codes, helping you avoid phishing pages, fake storefronts, and malicious redirects. All checks run locally in your browser: no links are uploaded, logged, or sent to any server.

This experimental build is designed for personal use and education. Always use your judgment and follow guidance from your bank, telecom provider, or local authorities when dealing with suspicious messages or payment requests.
