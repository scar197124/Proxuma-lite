About Proxuma Lite

Proxuma Lite is a local browser-based link analyzer designed to help you inspect URLs without uploading them to a remote service.

What it checks

  • Protocol safety and suspicious schemes
  • Domain structure, spoofing, and obfuscation
  • Encoded or hidden redirect behavior
  • Login, billing, verification, and scam language
  • Dangerous file extensions and double-extension tricks

What it does not do

  • It does not open the URL for you
  • It does not contact a server to scan
  • It does not guarantee a link is safe
  • It does not store your scan history
Privacy: Proxuma Lite runs analysis locally in your browser session. URLs are not sent anywhere by this app.
