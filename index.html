About Proxuma Lite

Proxuma Lite is a zero-cloud, zero-tracking, fully offline security scanner designed for people who value both safety and privacy.

How It Works

Proxuma extracts and analyzes the structure of a URL using a local intelligence engine that includes:

  • Heuristic scoring and signature matching
  • Behavioral pattern detection and threat archetype classification
  • Offline reasoning logic with confidence scoring
  • Explainable results with human-readable summaries

It evaluates the domain, protocol, path, redirect behavior, encoding patterns, and hidden intent signals, then produces a clear verdict such as: Safe, Low Risk, Medium Risk, High Risk, Dangerous, or Critical Threat.

No Data Leaves Your Device

Proxuma Lite does not:

  • Send URLs to a server
  • Log your scans or store history
  • Collect identities or analytics
  • Build profiles or share data

Everything runs inside your browser, privately and quietly. Proxuma Lite gives you the intelligence of a modern threat engine without the surveillance of one.

You own your safety. You own your data. Proxuma Lite keeps it that way.
