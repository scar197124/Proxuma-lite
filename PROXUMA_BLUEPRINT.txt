PROXUMA SYSTEM BLUEPRINT v1.0

CORE RULE:
Each Proxuma tier represents a different depth of clarity, not just more features.

---

PROXUMA LITE (Gateway Layer)
Purpose: Fast, everyday scanning
- QR Scanner: YES
- UI: Clean, accessible
- Output: Risk score + simple explanation
- No deep breakdowns
- Tone: Quick clarity

---

PROXUMA SENSE (Clarity Layer)
Purpose: Understanding why
- QR Scanner: NO
- UI: Minimal, focused
- Output: Deep explanation, educational
- Step-by-step reasoning
- Tone: Calm intelligence

---

PROXUMA SHIELD (Deep Analysis Layer)
Purpose: Full intelligence engine
- QR Scanner: YES (enhanced)
- UI: Structured, analytical
- Output:
  - Risk score
  - Threat classification
  - Confidence scoring
  - Technical breakdown
  - Suggestions
- Tone: Complete system awareness

---

GLOBAL RULES:
- Privacy-first always
- No cloud dependency
- Explain before warning
- Never promise perfect safety

---

DETECTION PHILOSOPHY:
“Does this increase clarity without reducing user control?”

---

USAGE:
Attach this file to any Proxuma project.
When analyzed, the system should:
1. Identify which tier it is
2. Apply the correct rules
3. Maintain consistency across builds
