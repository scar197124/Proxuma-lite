# Art of Creators — Proxuma Lite Identity Layer

## Core idea

Proxuma Lite should feel calm, private, sharp, and protective. It is not fear-based security. It is quiet intelligence that helps a person pause before trusting a link.

## Voice

- Clear before clever
- Calm before dramatic
- Explain the reason, not just the warning
- Never promise perfect safety
- Respect the user’s privacy and control

## Product phrases

- Offline Link Intelligence
- Scan before you trust
- Privacy-first by design
- Your link stays with you
- Explainable security, no surveillance

## Visual direction

- Dark mode should feel like a secure terminal, but still readable
- Green accent = local/private/active
- Risk colors should be useful, not overwhelming
- Results should feel like a diagnosis card, not a scare screen

## Creator rule

Every Proxuma feature should answer one question:

“Does this give the user more clarity without taking away their privacy?”

If yes, it belongs. If not, it waits.
